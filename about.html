About Us

Delicious house is a family-owned business that has been making delicious, high-quality pastries since 2005. Our mission is to provide our customers with the best pastries esepecially cakesusing only the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients.

Our pastry chef, Emory Timothy, has over 15 years of experience in the pastry industry and she is known for her creative and innovative pastry creations. She has won numerous awards for her pastries and is constantly experimenting with new flavors and techniques to bring our customers the best pastries possible.

Our Values

  • Quality: We believe in using only the freshest, highest-quality ingredients to make our pastries.
  • Creativity: We are constantly experimenting with new flavors and techniques to bring our customers the best pastries possible.
  • Community: We believe in supporting our local community by sourcing our ingredients from local farmers and suppliers.
